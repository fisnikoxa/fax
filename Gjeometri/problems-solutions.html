Problem 1

  • a) Point E should be in the first octant and point F symmetric to point E with respect to plane œÄ‚ÇÉ
  • b) Point E should be in the fourth octant and F symmetric to point E with respect to plane œÄ‚ÇÇ

Solution

Part a) Point E in octant I with equal distances from projection planes:

If E has equal distances from all three projection planes, then E(a, a, a) where a > 0.

Let E(3, 3, 3). Point F is symmetric to E with respect to plane œÄ‚ÇÉ (yz-plane).

Projections:

  • E': (3, 3) on œÄ‚ÇÅ, E'': (3, 3) on œÄ‚ÇÇ, E''': (3, 3) on œÄ‚ÇÉ
  • F': (-3, 3) on œÄ‚ÇÅ, F'': (-3, 3) on œÄ‚ÇÇ, F''': (3, 3) on œÄ‚ÇÉ

Problem 2

Draw the projections and determine in which octant each of the points A(3, 2, 4), B(2, -3, 2), C(-2, 5, 4), D(-4, -2, 5), E(-4, -2, -3) is located!

Solution

Octants are determined by the signs of coordinates (x, y, z):

  • Octant I: (+, +, +) - bottom right, above œÄ‚ÇÅ
  • Octant II: (+, -, +) - top right, above œÄ‚ÇÅ
  • Octant III: (-, -, +) - top left, above œÄ‚ÇÅ
  • Octant IV: (-, +, +) - bottom left, above œÄ‚ÇÅ
  • Octant V: (+, +, -) - bottom right, below œÄ‚ÇÅ
  • Octant VI: (+, -, -) - top right, below œÄ‚ÇÅ
  • Octant VII: (-, -, -) - top left, below œÄ‚ÇÅ
  • Octant VIII: (-, +, -) - bottom left, below œÄ‚ÇÅ

Answers:

  • A(3, 2, 4): (+, +, +) ‚Üí Octant I
  • B(2, -3, 2): (+, -, +) ‚Üí Octant II
  • C(-2, 5, 4): (-, +, +) ‚Üí Octant IV
  • D(-4, -2, 5): (-, -, +) ‚Üí Octant III
  • E(-4, -2, -3): (-, -, -) ‚Üí Octant VII

Problem 3

Given point A(A'', A'''). Determine the first projection A' of point A, when:
  • a) When A is located in octant I
  • b) A is located in octant II

Solution

If given A''(x, z) and A'''(y, z), then A' = (x, y).

Part a) A in octant I (x > 0, y > 0, z > 0):

  • A' = (x, y) where x > 0 and y > 0
  • The first projection A' lies in quadrant I (bottom right) of plane œÄ‚ÇÅ

Part b) A in octant II (x < 0, y > 0, z > 0):

  • A' = (x, y) where x < 0 and y > 0
  • The first projection A' lies in quadrant IV (bottom left) of plane œÄ‚ÇÅ

Example: If A''(3, 4) and A'''(2, 4):

  • Part a: A in octant I ‚Üí A(3, 2, 4), so A'(3, 2) in quadrant I (bottom right)
  • Part b: A in octant II ‚Üí A(-3, 2, 4), so A'(-3, 2) in quadrant IV (bottom left)
